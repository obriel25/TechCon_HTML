TechCon 2024

Our Mission

The goal of TechCon is to support learning, innovation, and open access to technology knowledge.
The program brings together developers, students, researchers, and companies.
You get direct exposure to new tools, ideas, and practical solutions.

TechCon promotes skill growth through workshops and guided sessions.
You learn through small projects, peer reviews, and hands on tasks.
Every session supports clear outcomes for your work and learning path.
